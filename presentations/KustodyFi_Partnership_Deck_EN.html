Settlement Architecture

T+1 Anchor with T+0 Instant Access

Layer Settlement Mechanism Use Case
T+0 Instant Real-time spot, on-chain settlement Retail FX, instant conversion
T+1 Next day TWAP fixing → Exchange benchmark rate Corporate treasury, accounting valuation
T+N Futures expiry Dated futures → Term structure Hedging, forward FX exposure

Why T+1 Anchor (not T+0)?

  • TWAP/VWAP requires a full trading day to compute
  • Accounting valuation needs a fixed, auditable reference rate
  • DF curve anchor must be deterministic, not real-time

vs. Traditional FX (T+2)

  • No bank holidays — 365-day calendar
  • 1 day faster settlement
  • 24/7 trading, no market close
